Grand Canyon - located in Arizona and it is one of the great tourist attractions in the United States. Visitors standing on the rim can see the canyon floor below and they can look out for the ridges and cliffs that's so widespread. It cuts deep into the landscape, creating dramatic cliff walls and ledges. Its overwhelming size and its colorful landscape offers visitors amazing view of the scenery. If you want to see the whole view of the Grand Canyon, consider signing up for a helicopter tour.


Niagara Falls - A group of 3 waterfalls between Ontario, Canada and New York, USA and it is one of the most popular waterfalls in the world. The largest of the 3 falls is called Horshoe Falls, the second is American Falls, and the third is Bridal Veil Falls. It is famed for its beauty and being a source of hydroelectric power. The water from Lake Eerie flows into Lake Ontario via these waterfalls.


Disney World - A mega amusement park located in Orlando, Florida. It is the top family attraction in America and became a popular staple in the American culture. It comprises of 4 theme parks, 2 water parks, 27 themed resort hotels, 9 non-Disney hotels, golf courses, camping resort, and a shopping center.
