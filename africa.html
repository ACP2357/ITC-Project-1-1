Pyramids of Giza, Egypt - The most iconic structures out of Egypt and usually the first thing the tourist go to when they land there. They're the oldest and the last standing architecture that's still intact out of the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World. These pyramids served as the tomb for the pharoahs back in Ancient Egypt.


Mount Kilimanjaro, Tanzania - A dormant volcano in Tanzania and it is also the African continent's highest point at 5,895 meters. Most people visit here to climb the summit and the best period to do it is between June and October, the dry season. It is popular for tourists because the journey doesn't involve mountaineering gear and climbing expertise. It also doesn't have extreme weather while climbing the summit.


Serengeti National Park - A national park and a wildlife refuge located in north-central Tanzania. It is well known for the largest annual migration of animals at more than 1.5 million of them crossing over. The best months for viewing wildlife in the park are between June and September.
