Sydney Opera House - A performing arts center in Sydney, Australia. It is one of the most famous and distinctive buildings of the 20th century architecture due to it being shaped like billowing sails of a ship. It is also part of the UNESCO World Heritage Sites. Inside, you can go there to enjoy the performances or join a guided tour as it contains theaters,cinema, concert hall, and exhibition rooms.


Kangaroo Island - The 3rd largest island in Australia. It is a popular tourist destination due to the untouched landscapes which makes incredible scenery and having an abundance of wildlife. It has huge dunes, cliffs, caves systems, and amazing rock formations. On the shore, you can find kangaroos while on the trees you can find koalas. In its crystal-clear waters, you can find penguins, dolphins, and sea lions. Due to its landscape, it is perfect for all sorts of outdoor activities such as hiking, sandboarding, and diving.


Blue Mountains National Park - It is a mountain range located in New South Wales, Australia. This park protects 664,000 acres of wilderness. When you visit it, you can explore forest, waterfalls, gorges, mountains, plateaus, Aboriginal rock paintings, and 140 km of hiking trails. Its most popular attraction is the Three Sisters, a unique rock formation found in the mountain. Hiking, rock climbing, mountain biking, and horseback riding are the usual outdoor activities you can do in the park.
