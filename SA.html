Galapagos Islands, Ecuador - An archipelgo of volcanic islands in the Pacific Ocean. The islands were known for having a large amount of endemic life and it helped Charles Darwin develop his theory of evolution via natural selection when he went there as part of a survey expedition. If you want to see rare animals then definitely go here. Go to El Chato Tortoise Reseve or San Rancho Primicias in Santa Cruz Island to see the Galapagos tortoises. When it comes to volcanoes, head to Isabella Island to see the Sierra Negra Volcano.


Machu Picchu, Peru - A UNESCO World Heritage site and the most prominent representation of the Inca civilization, it is nestled on an almost 8000 ft. mountain ridge. It served as a palace for the emperor, a citadel, and a place for religious ceremonies. You can go there by hiking the Inca Trail or by taking a train to Aguas Calientes.
