Eiffel Tower - Paris,France - The global cultural icon of France. The tower has 3 levels which houses gift shops, cafes,history exhibits of the tower, restaurants, and several observation decks. The first 2 levels can be accessed by stairs or a lift, but the 3rd one can only be accessed via an elevator.


Venice Canals, Italy - Venice has over 150 canals connecting 118 islands via bridges and walkways. It has alleyways, passageways, hidden courtyards, and examples of Gothic and Renaissance architecture lining up the banks of the canals. Much of the arhitecture can be seen by being on a gondola sightseeing tour under the Grand Canal.


Louvre Museum, France - The largest and most visited art museum in the world.Outside the museum, it is encased in a glass and metal pyramid structure called the Louvre Pyramid. Here, it houses some of the most popular art works in history such as Mona Lisa, The Coronation of Napoleon, and Liberty Leading the People. Overall, it contains 380,000 objects and about 35,000 are on permanent display.
