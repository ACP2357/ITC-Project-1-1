Boracay, Philippines - One of the most popular tourist destinations in the Philippines. It is known for its white sand beaches. You can find wide variety of places to stay, lots of souvenir shops, and a good amount of fun water activities. On the northern part of the island, there is Puka Shell beach, a good spot for shell collecting. If you want snorkelling, go to Tambisaan beach. As for the best view of the island from the top, you can go Mount Luho.


Bangkok, Thailand - It is filled with temples and shopping malls which makes the contrast of the setting interesting. Here, you'll find one of the biggest street markets in the world and as well as a number of floating markets. Since this city is so vibrant and dynamic, it constantly buzzes with energy and travellers should prepare for that to avoid getting overwhelmed.




Kathmandu, Nepal - The capital of Nepal. It is the home of Boudhanath,the holiest buddhist site and a part of UNESCO World Heritage site, and the Swayambhu, one of the oldest temples in Nepal and the place where you can find monkeys roaming around and interacting with the tourists. Climbers who want to reach Mount Everest arrives here first for preparation.
